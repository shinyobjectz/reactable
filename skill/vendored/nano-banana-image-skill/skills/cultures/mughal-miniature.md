# Mughal Miniature

## Use when

Use when the piece should reflect Mughal miniature painting cues: jewel tones, delicate ornament, profile grace, garden order, and refined court detail.

## Signature traits

- fine ornamental detail with hierarchy
- jewel-toned textiles and architectural richness
- profile or semi-profile pose logic
- garden, court, or palace organization

## Prompt levers

- decide whether the scene is intimate portrait, court tableau, hunting scene, or architectural view
- let gold detailing and textile pattern support focal hierarchy
- avoid overcrowding every inch with equal visual weight

## Examples

### Example 1 — Court tableau

Ruler seated on a patterned carpet beneath scalloped arches with attendants and pietra dura details integrated carefully.

### Example 2 — Garden portrait

Royal figure in a formal garden with floral motifs, layered textiles, and controlled architectural framing.

## Avoid

- flattening the culture into generic luxury ornament
- western oil-paint lighting that erases miniature logic
