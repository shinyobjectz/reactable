# Architecture Base

## Use when

Use for interiors, exteriors, archviz, city views, or built-environment storytelling where spatial logic and materials matter.

## Signature traits

- clear structural reading
- plausible scale and perspective
- material and glazing behavior anchored to light

## Prompt levers

- decide whether the emphasis is documentary, aspirational, or cinematic
- protect perspective integrity
- let people or props support scale only when necessary

## Examples

### Example 1 — Dusk exterior

Wide façade view with believable glazing reflections, interior practicals, and clean landscaping rhythm.

### Example 2 — Interior atrium

Balanced verticals, daylight spill, stone and brass behavior, and human scale cues placed carefully.

## Avoid

- warped geometry that breaks trust
- generic city clutter unrelated to the building story
