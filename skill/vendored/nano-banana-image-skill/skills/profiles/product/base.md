# Product Base

## Use when

Use for packshots, hero renders, ecommerce imagery, and campaign stills where geometry, label fidelity, and material response matter.

## Signature traits

- geometry and branding discipline
- surface-finish clarity
- controlled reflections and shadow anchors

## Prompt levers

- lock silhouette, label, cap, seam lines, and crop when editing
- choose ecommerce-clean versus luxury editorial lighting explicitly
- reserve background complexity for campaign shots, not packshots

## Examples

### Example 1 — Ecommerce packshot

Centered bottle, matte white sweep, crisp label readability, restrained soft shadow.

### Example 2 — Luxury editorial

Dark pedestal scene with jewel-toned backdrop, measured reflections, and premium rim-light separation.

## Avoid

- random props that hide the product
- changing geometry while claiming the product is preserved
