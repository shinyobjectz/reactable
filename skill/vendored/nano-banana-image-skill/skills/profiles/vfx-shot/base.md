# VFX Shot Base

## Use when

Use for shots that should feel like a frame from a visual effects pipeline: plate-aware, cinematic, and integration-conscious.

## Signature traits

- plate realism or believable hybrid realism
- integration of effects with perspective and light
- shot-specific composition rather than poster staging

## Prompt levers

- treat the camera as a real shot setup
- specify which element is live-action-like and which is augmented
- pair with Nuke composite or volumetric lighting when relevant

## Examples

### Example 1 — Creature plate augmentation

Street-level shot with inserted creature obeying contact shadow, depth haze, and practical spill.

### Example 2 — City extension

Rooftop plate expanded with distant towers and holographic ads matched to atmospheric perspective.

## Avoid

- poster-like flatness when the task needs shot realism
- FX layers that ignore the plate's light direction
