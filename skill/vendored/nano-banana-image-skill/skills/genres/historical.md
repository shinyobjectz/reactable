# Historical

## Use when

Use when era fidelity, costume discipline, architecture, tools, and cultural detail need explicit grounding.

## Signature traits

- era-appropriate materials and garments
- historically coherent architecture and props
- social and visual cues tied to the stated time and place
- measured color and ornament logic

## Prompt levers

- name the era and geography directly
- separate museum-faithful work from creative historical inspiration
- let wear, craftsmanship, and construction reveal period detail

## Examples

### Example 1 — Street scene

Late Edo riverside street with wood façades, paper lanterns, merchant banners, and clothing silhouettes consistent with the period.

### Example 2 — Court portrait

Imperial Mughal court figure on patterned carpet, jeweled garments, profile grace, garden architecture, and gold detailing appropriate to the setting.

## Avoid

- accidental modern fasteners, electronics, or materials
- mixing centuries without stating the blend intentionally
