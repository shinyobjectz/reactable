# Samsung Galaxy Nightography

## Use when

Use when the image should feel punchy, high-clarity, consumer-premium, and optimized for vivid night scenes.

## Signature traits

- punchier micro-contrast
- vivid night color bias
- clean edge definition
- bright nightlife readability

## Prompt levers

- keep highlights controlled while allowing vibrant sign color
- use strong nightlife practicals
- let surfaces feel glossy and energetic when appropriate

## Examples

### Example 1 — Concert crowd

High-energy night crowd with vivid LED spill, crisp subject separation, and controlled exposure.

### Example 2 — Night city portrait

Portrait against bright signage with rich color saturation and sharp foreground detail.

## Avoid

- muted art-film darkness if the goal is punchy consumer-night style
- film-grain nostalgia that clashes with polished device aesthetics
