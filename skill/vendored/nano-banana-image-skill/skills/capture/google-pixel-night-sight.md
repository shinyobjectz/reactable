# Google Pixel Night Sight

## Use when

Use when the image should feel computationally bright, clean, and detail-recovering in low light.

## Signature traits

- strong low-light detail recovery
- clean shadow readability
- natural but gently optimized color response
- urban handheld plausibility

## Prompt levers

- lean into clear texture recovery in shadows
- keep the viewpoint grounded and spontaneous
- preserve low-light realism without muddy darkness

## Examples

### Example 1 — Alley documentary frame

Narrow alley with readable wall texture, damp concrete, and balanced sign highlights.

### Example 2 — Night skyline from overlook

City view with lifted dark areas and strong point-light control.

## Avoid

- hyper-cinematic bloom that fights the computational feel
- fake DSLR super-shallow depth
