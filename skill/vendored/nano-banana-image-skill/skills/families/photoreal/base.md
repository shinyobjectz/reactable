# Photoreal Base

## Use when

Use for believable photographic imagery, grounded materials, and camera-led realism.

## Signature traits

- credible lens and depth behavior
- natural skin, fabric, and surface response to light
- real-world spatial logic
- controlled detail rather than synthetic oversharpening

## Prompt levers

- name the camera distance and lens feel
- ground the environment with real materials and practicals
- use lighting structure instead of prestige adjectives

## Examples

### Example 1 — Editorial portrait

Waist-up singer in rain-wet market light, 50mm feel, subtle skin texture, sodium and cyan mixed light.

### Example 2 — Environmental scene

Architectural dusk exterior with believable glazing reflections and physically plausible fog visibility.

## Avoid

- cartoonish edge glow unless intentionally surreal
- CG surface perfection that makes everything look fabricated
