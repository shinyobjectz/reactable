# Stylized 3D Base

## Use when

Use for designed 3D imagery with simplified forms, curated materials, and expressive shape language.

## Signature traits

- clean form simplification
- intentional material stylization
- playful or elegant proportion control
- readable render shapes over micro-detail noise

## Prompt levers

- specify whether the style leans toyetic, cinematic, or painterly
- keep edge design and color grouping tight
- let materials be stylized but internally consistent

## Examples

### Example 1 — Mascot portrait

Rounded forms, satin fabric, broad light shapes, restrained specular highlights, poster-safe composition.

### Example 2 — Explainer scene

Friendly classroom planet models with simplified geometry and clean label space.

## Avoid

- photoreal pore-level skin wording
- unmotivated realism mixed into every object
