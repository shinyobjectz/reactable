# Anime Base

## Use when

Use for polished, animation-led character or scene work with strong silhouette hierarchy and expressive staging.

## Signature traits

- clean silhouette readability
- controlled line edges and shape language
- graphic lighting rather than muddy realism
- selective detail concentration at face, hands, or hero props

## Prompt levers

- specify era or genre so anime styling does not become generic wallpaper
- choose whether the finish is theatrical feature-film, TV sharpness, or softer painted background style
- keep color grouping disciplined

## Examples

### Example 1 — Feature-film poster

Hero character framed against dawn clouds, disciplined rim light, painterly background depth, clear title-safe upper field.

### Example 2 — Action still

Mid-motion sword draw, strong diagonal staging, energy focused on the blade arc and expression rather than random debris.

## Avoid

- mixing in photoreal skin language unless deliberate hybridization is requested
- overloading every surface with texture
