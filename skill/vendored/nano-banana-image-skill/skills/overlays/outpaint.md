# Outpaint

## Use when

Use when the canvas must expand beyond current edges without breaking continuity.

## Signature traits

- edge continuity
- composition extension
- material and perspective preservation

## Prompt levers

- state which edges expand
- keep perspective and horizon stable
- decide whether the new space is quiet or content-bearing

## Examples

### Example 1 — Banner extension

Extend upward to create header space with sky and structural continuity.

### Example 2 — Social crop rescue

Expand left and right margins with environmental continuation while preserving the central figure.

## Avoid

- changing the original core composition unnecessarily
- introducing new focal subjects in the extension unless requested
