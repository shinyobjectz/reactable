# Volumetric Lighting

## Use when

Use when haze, fog, smoke, dust, or mist should reveal light beams, depth, and atmosphere.

## Signature traits

- visible light shafts or bloom through a medium
- enhanced depth layering
- cinematic atmosphere with motivated sources

## Prompt levers

- specify the atmosphere carrier: fog, smoke, dust, rain mist
- name the light sources cutting through the volume
- keep beam visibility plausible and not everywhere at once

## Examples

### Example 1 — Cathedral shaft

Morning beam slicing through dusty interior air and landing on stone floor patterns.

### Example 2 — Neon alley haze

Signage and practicals carving thin light through drizzle and street vapor.

## Avoid

- adding random god rays with no medium or source
