# YouTube Vlog

## Use when

Use for video cover art or vlog-style thumbnails that need one clear subject and strong glance legibility.

## Signature traits

- high click readability
- clear face or hero object emphasis
- simple supporting background
- title-safe side or top zones

## Prompt levers

- treat it like thumbnail-first design
- exaggerate focal hierarchy but keep it tasteful
- protect text against noisy backgrounds

## Examples

### Example 1 — Travel vlog cover

Host foregrounded against simplified cityscape with a bold but uncluttered title area.

### Example 2 — Tech review cover

Product large in frame, host reaction secondary, background abstracted into readable color fields.

## Avoid

- overly intricate scenes that collapse in thumbnail form
- tiny multi-line typography
