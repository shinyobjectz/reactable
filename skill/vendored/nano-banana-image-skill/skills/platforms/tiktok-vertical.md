# TikTok Vertical

## Use when

Use for tall, mobile-first imagery with a strong vertical read and central action band.

## Signature traits

- 9:16 vertical hierarchy
- center-weighted focal design
- quick top-to-bottom readability
- room for captions or overlays

## Prompt levers

- protect face or hero object in the central band
- avoid critical details at the extreme edges
- build for fast scroll impact

## Examples

### Example 1 — Host frame

Tall crop with face-forward energy and simplified background for title or caption overlays.

### Example 2 — Poster remix

Vertical event art with dominant upper title and midframe action band.

## Avoid

- horizontal compositions lazily cropped tall
- important text buried in the lower UI-heavy region
