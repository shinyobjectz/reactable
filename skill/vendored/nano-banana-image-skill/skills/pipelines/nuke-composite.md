# Nuke Composite

## Use when

Use when the image should feel like a composited VFX shot: plate realism, integrated elements, and post-aware finishing.

## Signature traits

- plate-aware realism
- convincing integration of CG or effects into live-action logic
- controlled post-finish and selective glow
- attention to edge integration and depth layering

## Prompt levers

- use for vfx-shot or hybrid photoreal work
- specify what belongs to the plate versus the added element
- keep compositing believable rather than flashy by default

## Examples

### Example 1 — Creature insertion

Photoreal city street plate with creature integrated through matching light direction, atmospheric depth, and contact shadow logic.

### Example 2 — Sci-fi matte augmentation

Existing rooftop scene extended with distant holographic towers and plate-matched haze.

## Avoid

- using Nuke as a synonym for 'cool'
- CG edges so clean they stop feeling comped into a plate
