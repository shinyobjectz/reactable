# YouTube Thumbnail Examples

Ready-to-use thumbnail prompts. All examples generate 1280x720px (16:9) by default.

---

## Tech Review Thumbnail

**Use case:** Gadget reviews, software demos, product unboxings.

```
YouTube thumbnail for iPhone 16 Pro review:

SUBJECT: Person with excited/surprised expression, eyes wide, slight open smile
positioned on left 2/3 of frame, looking at camera with engaging eye contact

PRODUCT: iPhone 16 Pro prominently displayed on right side
with subtle glow/rim light effect, approximately 30% of total frame width

TEXT: "WORTH IT?" in bold sans-serif, 72pt
white text with black outline (2px), top center position

BACKGROUND: Blurred dark tech-themed bokeh, high contrast with subject

TECHNICAL: 1280x720px, 16:9, high saturation and contrast, vibrant colors
```

**Expected result:** The person-on-left + product-on-right layout is the most proven CTR composition for review content. High saturation makes it pop in mobile feeds.

## Before/After Split

**Use case:** Transformation content, tutorials showing results, comparison videos.

```
Split-screen before/after YouTube thumbnail:

LAYOUT: Vertical split 50/50, clear white dividing line
- Left side: "BEFORE" label in bold red at top, desaturated/darker image, problem state
- Right side: "AFTER" label in bold green at top, vibrant/bright image, solution state

CENTER: Large "VS" text on dividing line, or transformation arrow pointing right

TEXT: Main headline "AI changed everything" spanning both sides at bottom
in white bold sans-serif with dark shadow

VISUAL CUES: Red X symbol on before side, green checkmark on after side

TECHNICAL: 1280x720px, perfect 50/50 symmetry, high contrast, mobile-optimized
```

**Expected result:** Strong visual contrast between the two halves drives curiosity. Red/green color coding makes the before/after relationship immediately clear even at small sizes.

## Tutorial / How-To

**Use case:** Step-by-step guides, explainer videos, educational content.

```
Tutorial thumbnail for "How to build a chatbot in 10 minutes":

SUBJECT: Person in casual t-shirt pointing confidently at laptop screen showing code
Expression: Helpful, slightly surprised, approachable and energetic

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Laptop screen showing colorful code (React/Python style)
- "10 MIN" badge in top right corner, accent orange color
- Step numbers "1-2-3" visible as graphic element

TEXT: "CHATBOT in 10 min" in bold white text, high contrast against background
Numbers "10" oversized for emphasis

BACKGROUND: Clean home office or desk setup, not distracting, slight blur

TECHNICAL: 1280x720px, readable at 120px (mobile search result size), high contrast
```

**Expected result:** Time-based hooks ("10 min") consistently perform well in educational content. The pointing gesture draws attention to the screen and signals "look at this." Text must be large enough to read in YouTube's search thumbnails.
