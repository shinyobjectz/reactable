# Infographic Examples

Ready-to-use infographic prompts. Organized by layout type.

---

## Timeline Infographic

**Use case:** Company history, product roadmaps, historical overviews.

```
Clean horizontal timeline infographic: 'The Rise of Generative AI'
Showing 6 key milestones from 2017 to 2024.
Circular markers for each date, thin grey dividers between events.
Include minimal icons, short text blocks (max 2 lines each).
Gradient accents in blue-to-violet. Bold header at top in dark typography.
White background, symmetrical layout, even spacing between milestones.
Format: 16:9, 1920x1080px.
```

**Expected result:** Clean horizontal flow with equal spacing between events. White background makes it easy to embed in presentations. The gradient accent adds visual hierarchy without overwhelming the content.

> **Tip:** Keep milestone text to 5-7 words max. Longer text blocks make the timeline crowded and hard to read.

## Comparison Infographic

**Use case:** Product comparisons, pros/cons analysis, A vs B decisions.

```
Two-column comparison infographic: 'Agency vs In-house AI Team'
Left side: blue (#2563EB) theme - "Agency" - show 4 advantages with icons:
  speed of deployment, lower fixed costs, access to specialized tools, no hiring needed
Right side: violet (#7C3AED) theme - "In-house" - show 4 advantages with icons:
  full data control, deeper product context, long-term knowledge retention, custom integrations
Summary comparison row at bottom: "Best for: fast experiments" vs "Best for: core product AI"
Clean table grid, high contrast text, avoid gradients within cells.
Format: 1080x1080px (square for social media).
```

**Expected result:** Two-column structure is instantly scannable. Square format works on LinkedIn and Instagram. The summary row at the bottom helps readers who scroll directly to the conclusion.

## Statistical / KPI Dashboard

**Use case:** Business reports, data summaries, investor materials, annual reviews.

```
Data visualization infographic: 'AI Adoption in Polish Companies 2025'
Top section: 4 big-number KPI cards with key metrics -
  67% adopted AI tools, 3.2h saved per employee weekly, 41% report cost reduction, 89% plan to expand usage
Middle: Horizontal bar chart comparing 5 industries by AI adoption rate
Bottom: Donut chart showing breakdown of AI use cases (automation 38%, content 27%, analytics 22%, other 13%)
Professional business style, corporate palette (#0F172A dark background, #3B82F6 accent blue)
Clean typography, generous padding, all numbers sharp and legible
Format: 1920x1080px
```

**Expected result:** Dashboard-style layout with clear visual hierarchy. KPI cards at top provide the key takeaways for readers who won't read the whole piece. Bar + donut combination covers both comparison and composition data in one image.
