# Image Generation Examples

Ready-to-use generation prompts. Each example includes notes on expected output quality and use case.

---

## Product Photography - Minimal Studio

**Use case:** E-commerce listings, ads, social media product posts.

```
Professional product photograph of a matte black coffee grinder on a minimalist white marble surface.
Soft studio lighting from top-left creating subtle shadows.
Clean composition with negative space on right side for text placement.
Shot with macro lens, f/4, sharp focus on product details.
High-end advertising style, 4K resolution.
```

**Expected result:** Clean white-background style photo with professional lighting. The negative space on the right works well for adding price or headline text in post.

---

## Product Photography - Lifestyle/Mood

**Use case:** Premium products, Instagram content, brand visuals.

```
Glass perfume bottle floating on dark water with white gardenias and pale pink roses arranged around it.
Golden hour glow creates reflections and ripples across the water.
Ethereal and luxurious mood, high-end commercial photography.
Eye-level camera angle, shallow depth of field with soft bokeh background.
```

**Expected result:** Dramatic, editorial-style image with strong mood. The floating-on-water composition reads as luxury. Works best for perfumes, cosmetics, jewelry.

---

## Corporate Portrait / Headshot

**Use case:** LinkedIn profiles, team pages, speaker bios.

```
Professional half-length corporate portrait in studio setting.
Person in navy blue suit against pure white backdrop.
Minimal shadows, clean and polished look.
Natural skin tones, sharp focus on eyes.
LinkedIn-quality headshot, professional lighting, 2K resolution.
```

**Expected result:** Clean, professional headshot suitable for business use. White background makes it easy to place anywhere. Use with a reference image for best face accuracy.

---

## Cinematic Scene

**Use case:** Story illustrations, blog headers, editorial visuals.

```
35-year-old woman in a tailored charcoal trench coat walks along a rain-soaked Paris street at dusk.
Reflections of neon signs in puddles, shallow depth of field, bokeh from distant streetlights.
Film photography aesthetic, slight grain, muted color palette with pops of warm amber.
35mm lens, eye-level shot, cinematic composition.
```

**Expected result:** Atmospheric, editorial-quality scene. The rain and reflections add visual complexity. "Film photography aesthetic" triggers natural-looking color grading rather than overly saturated AI output.
